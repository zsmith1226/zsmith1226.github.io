(3)List additional suggestions/contributions for learning community
When creating my RDR, I found it very helpful that I had made a logically correct
pseudocode back in PL Asst 1. I felt I would have spent more time if the pseudocode
were incorrect or if I did not have it all. That being said, I would suggest
that even though it might not be necessary for some, that pseudocode, or diagrams
be made to assist with coding until more experience is gained.
